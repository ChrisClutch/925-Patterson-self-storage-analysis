Conversion Highlights

The West End section is being separated from the rest of the 185,000 SF complex with a new rated firewall, creating an independent property line and dedicated access. The 100,000 SF building yields 80,000 SF of net rentable storage across two floors, plus a large covered pull-in loading area for tenant convenience. The 30–34 ft clear heights allow for a full second story while maintaining comfortable corridor heights on both levels.

Competitive Advantage

Patterson Self Storage will be the only modern, purpose-converted facility in the central West Salem trade area. The nearest competitor, Public Storage on Glen Creek, is an older facility approximately 1 mile north with limited drive-up access. Its 94% occupancy signals strong unmet demand in the submarket.

Most other Salem storage options are located 3+ miles east across the Willamette River. Patterson serves as the convenient choice for West Salem's 23,559 immediate residents while also drawing from Salem's broader 181,000+ population — all within a short bridge crossing.

Investment Incentives
Tax Benefits & Grant Opportunities

This property sits within both a Federal Opportunity Zone and the West Salem Urban Renewal Area, providing a layered incentive structure for development investment.

Opportunity Zone

Defer and reduce capital gains taxes on investments held 5+ years. Investments held 10+ years pay zero capital gains on OZ appreciation. Ideal for 1031 exchange alternatives and long-term holds.

West Salem Redevelopment Grant

Up to $300,000 in matching grants (20% match requirement) for eligible building improvements. Investment must remain in the district at least 10 years. Applicable to interior and exterior permanent construction.
